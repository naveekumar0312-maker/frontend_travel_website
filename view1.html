Day 1

Cochin to Munnar

  • Pick up from Cochin and drive to Munnar.
  • Visit Cheeyappara & Valara Waterfalls, Tea Plantations, Spice Garden.
  • Reaching Munnar, rest for the day at the hotel.

Day 2

Munnar to Alleppey Houseboat

  • Munnar to Alleppey drive will take a minimum of 5 hrs, suggest leaving at 8 am.
  • Enjoy the backwaters, scenery, and village life for 21hrs in houseboats.
  • Late check-in may happen if delay in travel.
  • Lunch break for 1 hr, cruise till 5:30 pm.
  • Boat anchored overnight, morning cruise for 1 hr.
  • Categories: Deluxe, Premium, Luxury.
  • Welcome drink, tea, snacks, water, lunch, dinner, breakfast included.

Day 3

Alleppey to Cochin Drop

  • Visit Alleppey beach and drop at Cochin.
  • Cochin sightseeing: Fort Cochin, Navy Museum, St Francis Church.
  • Chinese Fishing Net, Santa Cruz Basilica.
  • Mattanchery Palace, Jewish Synagogue.
  • Marine Drive, boating, Hill Palace.
  • Tripunithura Hill Palace closed on Monday.
  • Dutch Palace closed Friday.
  • Synagogue closed Fri & Sat.
