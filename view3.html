Day 1

Cochin to Alleppey Houseboat

  • Drive to Alleppey and check-in to Houseboat at 12 Noon.
  • Houseboat will be stopped for lunch for 1hrs with the cruise till 5:30 pm.
  • The boat will be anchored till the next morning.
  • 1-hour cruise in the morning before check out at 9 am.
  • There are different categories of houseboats available such as Deluxe, Premium, and Luxury, where Deluxe is the basic category.
  • In the houseboat, you will be greeted with a welcome drink, tea, snacks, mineral water, lunch, dinner and breakfast.

Day 2

Alleppey to Munnar

  • The drive from Alleppey to Munnar requires a minimum of 5 hours.
  • If you have only one night stay at Munnar, start early from Alleppey so as to reach Munnar at the earliest for sightseeing on the same day.
  • If you have two nights at Munnar, start from Alleppey at your own time after visiting Alleppey Beach.
  • Munnar sightseeing can be done the next day.

Day 3

Munnar

  • Suggest starting at 9 am for visiting Eravikulam National park, followed by Tea Museum.
  • Post lunch, visit Mattuppetty dam, Echo point, Kundala Lake, Viewpoints, Pothamedu view point and Botanical Garden.
  • Note: The Eravikulam National Park tickets can be either booked in advance or purchased from the counter.
  • The park is closed from Feb to April due to the breeding time of Nilgiri Tahr.
  • Tea Museum is closed on Mondays.

Day 4

Munnar to Cochin drop

  • Munnar to Cochin drop
  • If time available, we provide complimentary Cochin sightseeing which includes fort cochin, the Navy museum, St Francis church, the Chinese fishing net, Santa Cruz basilica, Mattanchery palace, Jewish synagogue, Marine drive, Marine walkway, boating, Tripunitura hill palace.
  • You can find some time for shopping at Cochin.
  • Our representative will leave you at Cochin Airport/Railway station/Bus stand and will extend the necessary support there
  • Drive back to your hometown with the sweet memories of the Kerala trip with Paradise Holidays.
  • Hill palace opens from 9 AM to 5 PM and is closed on Mondays.
  • The Dutch palace is closed on Fridays.
  • The synagogue opens from 10 AM to 1 PM and 3 to 5 PM and is closed on Fridays and Saturdays.
